# Understanding Your Menstrual Cycle

Your menstrual cycle is a monthly process that prepares your body for possible pregnancy. Understanding your cycle can help you predict your next period and identify any irregularities.

## The Four Phases

1. **Menstruation** - The shedding of the uterine lining
2. **Follicular Phase** - Eggs develop in the ovaries
3. **Ovulation** - An egg is released
4. **Luteal Phase** - The body prepares for possible pregnancy

## How Tracking Helps

By tracking your cycle, you can:

- Predict your next period with greater accuracy
- Identify patterns and irregularities
- Plan activities around your cycle
